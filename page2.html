CONCEPT NOTE

This painting embodies my self-directed frustration for not excelling in painting. It symbolizes being emotionally intertwined with someone to a painful extent, akin to a profound loss. The wheelchair signifies my artistic limitations, while the water reflects the persistently haunting memories of that person.
