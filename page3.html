CONCEPT NOTE

I was never focused on self-representation but rather on finding a way of reimagining the nude. I wanted the body to be seen as a man experiences himself, rather than through the reflection of a mirror or others' eyes. By looking from a man's perspective, I want to represent the masculinity of showing oneself in a superior or bigger way. Showing a man in a nude sleeping position aims to convey the feelings of insecurity and vulnerability that a man experiences.
