ARTIST STATEMENT

My practice is based on my experience and interaction with people about their emotions, ideologies, beliefs, social values, certain moral conducts. I try to portray human behaviors in particular situations through my work. The title of my works is an entry point to my thoughts.

My works are informed by a variety of pictorial traditions such as surrealism, realism, Indian classical and Western Art and so on. I appropriate images from popular visual culture. I choose various materials according to the requirements of my work. I also use unconventional materials and try to push the possibilities to make new visuals to best communicate my ideas. After My Internship in traditional miniature painting in Jaipur, I tried to develop the technical aspect of certain works and developed a few works accordingly.

I also interact with people to make my work powerful. I like to go through a research and development process before executing each of my ideas.
